2. What are the different lift dispatch algorithms
If a building has 2 lifts :

Approach 1:
    Lift 1 will serve the even floors
    Lift 2 will serve the odd floors

Approach 2:
    Lift 1 will serve from 1 - 5
    Lift 2 will serve from 6 - 10
